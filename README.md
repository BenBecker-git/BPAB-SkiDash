# BPAB-SkiDash
Privacy Policy for Ski Dash
Ski Dash does not directly collect personal information from players.

The app may use third-party services such as ads and online leaderboard features. These services may process basic anonymous device information as needed for app functionality.

If a score is submitted to the leaderboard, player initials and score data may be stored and shown publicly inside the app.

Ski Dash is designed as a simple game experience and does not require accounts or personal profiles.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer.
